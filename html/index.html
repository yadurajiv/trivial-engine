Keep it Trivial, Stupid!

Trivial Engine is our solution to global warming, terrorism, poverty, racism and other world problems. We want to make the act of prototyping new game ideas so simple and uncomplicated - so trivial, that anyone, yes anyone, can make games.

Want Developer Preview?

We are working hard to get a public release schedule ready for the Trivial Engine. We need your help to do so quickly.

We will soon have the engine ready in a state where it can be actively used in a production pipeline and we want you to use it and tell us how you like the engine, and what you would like to be improved in the engine.

It will also give us insights in how the Trivial Engine adopts to your development cycles and processes, and how we can make that integration smoother.

If that sounds interesting to you, all we ask for is your email address below. We promise not to spam!
